Types of Visas

Discover the different visa and immigration process options available.

Italian Citizenship

Complete process for recognizing Italian citizenship through ancestry, including genealogical research and documentation.

EB-1

First preference category for individuals with extraordinary abilities in sciences, arts, education, business, or athletics.

EB-2

For professionals with advanced degrees or exceptional abilities. Includes the National Interest Waiver (NIW) option.

EB-3

Category for skilled workers, graduates, and unskilled workers with a permanent job offer in the USA.

T Visa

Designed for victims of human trafficking in the USA, allowing them to work and providing a possible pathway to permanent residency.

Green Card

The Green Card allows residency and work in the USA and can be obtained through various means, such as marriage or parental sponsorship.

Work Visas in the USA

There are different types of work visas in the USA, varying based on qualifications, experience, or investment.

Temporary Visas for Study and Work

These visas allow temporary study, work, or investment in the USA, depending on specific eligibility requirements.
